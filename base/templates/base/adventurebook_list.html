Adventure Books

"Treasure Island" by Robert Louis Stevenson

Follow the thrilling adventures of young Jim Hawkins as he discovers a treasure map and embarks on a perilous journey to a deserted island in search of buried pirate treasure. This timeless classic is filled with swashbuckling pirates, mutiny, and high-seas adventure.

"The Call of the Wild" by Jack London

Set during the Klondike Gold Rush, this gripping tale follows Buck, a domesticated dog who is stolen from his comfortable home and sold into the harsh life of an Alaskan sled dog. As Buck taps into his primal instincts, he ultimately embraces his wild ancestry.

"Into the Wild" by Jon Krakauer

This non-fiction book chronicles the true story of Christopher McCandless, a young man who abandons his possessions and savings to venture into the Alaskan wilderness. Krakauer delves into McCandless's motivations, adventures, and the ultimate tragedy of his journey.

"The Hobbit" by J.R.R. Tolkien

Bilbo Baggins, a reluctant hobbit, is swept into an epic quest to reclaim the lost Kingdom of Erebor from the fearsome dragon Smaug. Along the way, Bilbo encounters trolls, elves, and the creature Gollum, discovering his own bravery and cunning.

"Around the World in Eighty Days" by Jules Verne

Phileas Fogg, an English gentleman, wagers that he can circumnavigate the globe in just eighty days. Accompanied by his loyal servant Passepartout, Fogg faces numerous obstacles and adventures as he races against time to win the bet.

"The Alchemist" by Paulo Coelho

Santiago, a shepherd boy, dreams of discovering a hidden treasure located near the Egyptian pyramids. His journey across the desert teaches him about the importance of listening to his heart and following his dreams.

"The Lost City of Z" by David Grann

This non-fiction book recounts the true story of British explorer Percy Fawcett, who ventured into the Amazon in search of a fabled ancient city. Grann's gripping narrative brings to life the dangers and mysteries of the uncharted jungle.

"Jurassic Park" by Michael Crichton

Scientists create a theme park populated by cloned dinosaurs on a remote island. When the park's security systems fail, the dinosaurs break loose, leading to a thrilling and dangerous adventure for the visitors and staff trying to survive.

"The Three Musketeers" by Alexandre Dumas

In this swashbuckling adventure, young d'Artagnan travels to Paris to join the Musketeers of the Guard. Along with three legendary musketeersAthos, Porthos, and Aramishe becomes embroiled in political intrigues and duels, seeking justice and honor.

"Shantaram" by Gregory David Roberts

This novel follows the life of Lin, an Australian fugitive who escapes to the underworld of Bombay. As he navigates through the city's slums, mafia, and bustling life, Lin discovers love, loyalty, and redemption.

"Hatchet" by Gary Paulsen

Thirteen-year-old Brian Robeson survives a plane crash and finds himself alone in the Canadian wilderness with only a hatchet. His story of survival, resilience, and self-discovery captivates readers as Brian learns to live off the land.

"The Count of Monte Cristo" by Alexandre Dumas

Edmond Dants is wrongfully imprisoned and, after escaping from prison, he seeks revenge against those who betrayed him. This classic tale of adventure, justice, and retribution spans across various exotic locales and complex characters.
